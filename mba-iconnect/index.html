MBA iConnect 2025-2027

The MBA iConnect program at ITM University, Raipur, is a comprehensive two-year course designed to equip students with the skills needed for success in the ever-evolving business world.

  • Balanced Learning Approach: Integrates academic learning with hands-on experience through live projects, workshops, and industry visits, ensuring students gain practical knowledge.
  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Focuses on leadership, management skills, and real-world applications, aligning with the current needs of the business industry.
  • Experiential Learning Opportunities: Prioritizes internships, case studies, and group discussions to enhance practical skills and build confidence in students.
  • Career-Ready Foundation: Prepares students with strong business fundamentals and industry connections, facilitating a seamless transition into their professional careers.
  • Certification Courses: Integrated with Grant Thornton, Coursera, and Swayam (NPTEL), these industry-focused certifications enhance employability. They cover 10-20% of the curriculum with faculty support for better understanding.
  • Guest Lectures: Experts conduct 4 to 10-hour workshops, offering hands-on experience integrated into regular classes for practical learning.
  • Immersion Program: High-performing students get opportunities to study at top institutions in India and abroad for deeper subject insights.

ELIGIBILITY AND HOW TO APPLY

To be eligible for the BBA (Logistics) (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, candidates must have a 10+2 qualification in any discipline with a minimum of 45% marks. If seats remain vacant, the required percentage may be reduced by 5% with the approval of the Vice-Chancellor.

ABOUT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

With over 34 years of academic excellence, ITM Group of Institutions founded ITM University, Raipur, in 2012, transforming higher education in Central India. Situated in Raipur, a key industrial and educational center, the university provides students with outstanding career prospects in a dynamic global environment.

ITM University, Raipur, offers industry-oriented education with advanced infrastructure and a faculty that blends academic expertise with real-world experience. Its vibrant, eco-friendly campus fosters holistic development by integrating rigorous academics with diverse extracurricular activities.

In alignment with the National Education Policy 2020, the university focuses on skill-based learning, entrepreneurship, and hands-on training. Students gain practical exposure through moot courts, debates, conclaves, and interactive workshops, ensuring they are career-ready.

With diverse programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, empowers students with the knowledge and skills to thrive in their fields. By fostering innovation, leadership, and professional excellence, the university is shaping the next generation of industry leaders.
