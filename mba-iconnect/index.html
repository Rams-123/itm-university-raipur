With over 34 years of academic excellence, ITM Group of Institutions founded ITM University, Raipur, in 2012, transforming higher education in Central India. ITM University, Raipur, offers industry-oriented education with advanced infrastructure and a faculty that blends academic expertise with real-world experience. Its vibrant, eco-friendly campus fosters holistic development by integrating rigorous academics with diverse extracurricular activities.

With diverse programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, empowers students with the knowledge and skills to thrive in their fields. By fostering innovation, leadership, and professional excellence, the university is shaping the next generation of industry leaders.
