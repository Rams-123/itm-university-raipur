Program Highlights
Industry-Ready Curriculum with Practical Depth

The M.Sc. in Microbiology program at ITM University, Raipur, has 12 core subjects, 6 electives, and skill-based courses to ensure a well-rounded, future-ready education. With a blend of theory, lab work, and advanced topics like Recombinant DNA Technology, students gain a competitive edge in academics and industry.

Mandatory 6-Month Internship & Hands-On Training

Students gain immersive real-world experience through a compulsory 6-month internship in the final semester, alongside short-term summer training, industrial visits, and science exhibitions. This practice-rich approach ensures that students are career-ready and confident in real-world microbiological settings.

100% Placement Assistance with Top Industry Linkages

With a strong network of recruiters from biotech, pharma, healthcare, and research sectors, ITM offers 100% placement support. Students benefit from resume-building sessions, mock interviews, and campus drives, with top packages reaching up to ₹10 LPA and an average of ₹4 LPA.

State-of-the-Art Labs & World-Class Campus

The 25-acre green campus features cutting-edge microbiology labs equipped with BSL-II, spectrophotometers, centrifuges, and more. The infrastructure includes a vast library, digital tools, and modern classrooms to foster a superior academic environment.

Certifications & Global Learning Opportunities

Students can enhance their academic portfolio with optional certifications in Agri-Drone Piloting, Mushroom Cultivation, and Analytical Instrumentation, in collaboration with the industry. Access to MOOC platforms like SWAYAM (NPTEL) allows students to customize their learning journey for global relevance.

ABOUT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

With a rich legacy of over 34 years in education, the ITM Group of Institutions established ITM University, Raipur, in 2012, introducing a progressive and transformative learning environment to the heart of Central India. Situated in Raipur - a growing center for industry and education - the university offers students a strategic platform for building successful careers in today’s globalized world.

ITM University, Raipur, is committed to delivering future-focused education supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and a faculty comprising seasoned academicians and industry experts. The lush, eco-conscious campus nurtures holistic student development, blending academic rigor with dynamic cultural, technical, and co-curricular experiences.

Aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, the university embraces a skills-driven, entrepreneurial, and hands-on approach to learning. From moot courts and industry conclaves to live training sessions and debates, students are consistently exposed to real-world scenarios that build their confidence and professional readiness.

Offering diverse programs across Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, is dedicated to nurturing innovative, purpose-driven leaders equipped to excel in a rapidly evolving world.
