About B.Com. (Hons.)

The B.Com. (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, is a comprehensive 3-year full-time program (with an optional 4th year to obtain a B.Com. Honors degree) designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the ever-evolving business world.

  • Strong Foundation in Commerce: Combines academic rigor with practical insights to build a deep understanding of commerce principles and practices.
  • Experiential Learning Opportunities: Features internships, live projects, and workshops to provide hands-on experience and enhance real-world problem-solving skills.
  • Skill Development Focus: Emphasizes analytical thinking, communication, and decision-making to prepare students for diverse career opportunities in commerce and related fields.
  • Career-Ready Pathway: Prepares graduates with industry-relevant expertise and professional connections for a seamless transition into the workforce or advanced studies.

ELIGIBILITY AND HOW TO APPLY

To be eligible for the BBA (Logistics) (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, candidates must have a 10+2 qualification in any discipline with a minimum of 45% marks. If seats remain vacant, the required percentage may be reduced by 5% with the approval of the Vice-Chancellor.

ABOUT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

With over 34 years of academic excellence, ITM Group of Institutions founded ITM University, Raipur, in 2012, transforming higher education in Central India. Situated in Raipur, a key industrial and educational center, the university provides students with outstanding career prospects in a dynamic global environment.

ITM University, Raipur, offers industry-oriented education with advanced infrastructure and a faculty that blends academic expertise with real-world experience. Its vibrant, eco-friendly campus fosters holistic development by integrating rigorous academics with diverse extracurricular activities.

In line with the National Education Policy 2020, the university prioritizes skill-based learning, entrepreneurship, and hands-on exposure. Through moot courts, debates, conclaves, and interactive training, students acquire practical experience, ensuring they are prepared for the job market.

Offering diverse programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, empowers students with the expertise to thrive in their careers. The university is shaping the next generation of industry leaders by nurturing innovation, leadership, and professional excellence.
