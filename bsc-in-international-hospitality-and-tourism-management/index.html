ABOUT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

With over 34 years of academic excellence, ITM Group of Institutions established ITM University, Raipur, in 2012, setting new benchmarks in higher education in Central India. Strategically located in Raipur, a prominent industrial and educational hub, the university offers students strong career prospects in a rapidly changing global landscape.

ITM University, Raipur, provides industry-focused education, supported by modern infrastructure and a faculty that blends academic expertise with real-world experience. Its eco-friendly and dynamic campus fosters all-round development by integrating rigorous academics with a wide range of extracurricular activities.

Aligned with the National Education Policy 2020, the university emphasizes practical learning, entrepreneurship, and skill development. Students gain hands-on experience through moot courts, debates, conclaves, and interactive workshops, ensuring they are job-ready.

With diverse programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, equips students with the expertise to thrive in their careers. By fostering innovation, leadership, and professional excellence, the university is shaping future industry leaders.
