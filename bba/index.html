ABOUT ITM VOCATIONAL UNIVERSITY

The ITM Group of Institutions, established in 1991, is known for its vibrant student community and a strong focus on academic excellence and personal development. ITM provides a unique learning environment supported by experienced full-time and visiting faculty.

The institution is committed to becoming a global leader in professional education by offering futuristic and comprehensive programs of international standards, fostering discipline, and promoting social responsibility.

ITM Vocational University (ITM VUG), Gujarat, is a part of this esteemed group and was established in 2014 as a Private University approved by the Government of Gujarat. It is India’s first truly Vocational University, integrating general and vocational education into higher education programs.

This innovative approach ensures students gain employable skills, professional qualifications, and international recognition, preparing them for a dynamic career.
