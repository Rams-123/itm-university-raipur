HOSTEL FACILITIES AT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

ITM University offers comfortable and secure hostel accommodation for both male and female students on a first-come, first-served basis. Hostels are designed as apartment-style flats to provide a homely environment, focusing on cleanliness and convenience.

Hostel Key Features

Room Sharing Options

Rooms are available on a 2 and 3-sharing basis.

Mess & Canteen

On-campus and in-hostel dining with vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals: breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner on a fixed menu.

Drinking Water

RO-purified drinking water and water coolers for cold water are available.

Recreation

Separate recreation rooms with TVs for boys and girls; indoor and outdoor sports like carrom, chess, badminton, and cricket.

News & Information

Hindi and English newspapers are available in hostel offices.

Health & Safety

  • First-aid and primary medicines available.
  • 24/7 electricity, water supply, and security (male & female guards).
  • CCTV surveillance throughout the premises.
  • Vehicle on standby for medical emergencies.

Maintenance & Cleanliness

  • Daily housekeeping service.
  • Regular pest control in rooms and common areas.
  • Dedicated in-house maintenance team.

Transport

Shuttle service between the hostel and the campus.

Supervision

Full-time residential wardens (male and female) ensure safety and discipline.

EXTRACURRICULAR & SPORTS ACTIVITIES AT ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

At ITM University, student development goes beyond academics. The university focuses on four key pillars of growth

Extracurricular Engagement

Extracurricular activities allow students to showcase their talents in sports, cultural events, and recreational pursuits.

LIBRARY FACILITY

ITM University, Raipur Library caters to the teaching, learning and research needs of the academic community and its resources are consulted by scholars from all over the ITM group of institutions. At present, the library holds total collections over 17,631 books and 70 National and International print periodicals. Apart from the online Journals, Books provided by DELNET, ProQuest, Manupatra, BCL etc. and the library has purchased license to access more than 5000 online Science, Technology, Hotel Management, Architecture, Law research journals and over 5000 e-books available to support research activity and CD versions also available for the users.

Of late, the ITM University, Raipur Library has been modernized to provide computerized services to academic community at large. The Library is automated with integrated library software package and modernized with latest barcode-based automation system that facilitates Acquisition, Classification, Cataloguing, Circulation, Serial Control, User Registration, Transaction History, OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue), Shelve Management, Report, Support system. This technology/software offers fastest, easiest, most efficient way to track, locate and manage library materials. The basic aim of the library is to position itself as one of the leading libraries in India. The library also aims to provide the academic community with access to rich and relevant information resources to support their research, teaching and learning activities.

Services and Facilities

  • Circulation (Books Borrowing)
  • Reference Services
  • Online Public Access Catalogue (Information Desk)
  • E-Resources (e-Books, e-Journals, e-thesis etc.)
  • Print Periodicals (Journals/Magazines etc.)
  • Document Delivery Services through email and WhatsApp
  • Reprography/Scanning/Printing
  • Reading Zone for Faculty/Student & Staff
  • Book Bank Facility
  • Content Management Services through Institutional Repository
  • Inter-Library Loan (ITM Group & DELNET)
  • Library user awareness program including current awareness services
  • Remote Access to e-resources

E-Library Rules

  1. Identity Card/Library Borrower’s Card is mandatory for using E-Library facility.
  2. The use of the E-Library Facility is on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  3. Maximum use of E-Library Facility to students is 1 hour per day. For extended access, contact the senior librarian and justify your need.
  4. Silence must be observed at all times. Group discussions and phone calls are strictly prohibited.
  5. Chewing gum, food, or drinks are not allowed inside the E-Library. Violations may result in heavy penalties.
  6. Recreational activities such as playing games, video streaming, chatting, and accessing social media are strictly prohibited.
  7. Installing or running unauthorized software is not permitted.
  8. Accessing or downloading unwanted or inappropriate content is not allowed.
  9. Unlawful activities like hacking or tampering with system settings are strictly forbidden.
  10. Do not tamper with any equipment. For technical issues, please consult library staff.
  11. E-Library staff reserve the right to ask violators of these rules to leave the premises.
  12. Please cooperate with E-Library staff at all times.

5. Membership cards are non-transferable.

8. Maintain silence; no loud discussions or disturbances.

10. Check circulation details and reclaim your borrower’s card after returns.

13. Library staff may check materials at exit — cooperate.

14. Users are liable for damages/losses; pay double cost + fine + processing charges.

15. Reference books need written permission to issue.

16. Renewal allowed only if not reserved, with book’s physical presence.

17. Late fines are non-negotiable.

18. Do not reshelve books; leave on tables for staff.

19. Software installation/removal in e-library is prohibited — ask staff for help.

20. Library is under CCTV surveillance — follow rules.

21. Grievances must be written in the complaint register.

CANTEEN FACILITY

ITM University Canteen provides best meal services to the students with nominal charges with proper sanitary conditions and preventing adulterated food. The canteen is located inside the university campus. The hostellers and day scholars are provided meals by assuring food safety and quality.

The Management of the Canteen is directly under the control of University Administration. Menu will be planned by the assigned team of Management and instructions will be given to the Contractor and that will be under the supervision of university administration team.

Suggestions, complaints and problems of the food consumers are taken into consideration by administration team committee take corrective measures to ensure the availability, safety and quality of food services.

One common eating facility (canteen facility) is been provided in the campus for day scholars, hostellers, guests, teaching and non-teaching staff of the campus. The management is fully committed to keep the quality of the canteen for the utmost benefit of all concerned.
