ITM BA (PSYCHOLOGY) (HONS.) 2025-28

The BA (Psychology) (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, is a dynamic three-year undergraduate course with an optional fourth year for an honors degree, aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. This full-time program will nurture future psychologists and behavioral science professionals through interdisciplinary learning, real-world exposure, and research excellence.

  • Career-Centric Curriculum: Combines core psychological theories with practical applications across mental health, counseling, HR, and research, ensuring students are job-ready for diverse psychology-related roles.
  • Interdisciplinary & Contemporary Focus: Introduces students to specialized and emerging domains like Forensic Psychology, Neuropsychology, and Sports Psychology, offering a future-forward learning experience.
  • Real-World Learning Integration: Incorporates practical projects, case studies, and interactions with industry experts to connect classroom concepts with real-life applications.
  • Research & Inquiry-Driven Pedagogy: Encourages original research, critical thinking, and scientific inquiry through continuous faculty mentorship and engagement in live research projects.
  • Immersive Internship Experience: Mandatory internships across varied professional settings provide hands-on exposure, professional networks, and the confidence to thrive in psychological practice.

  • Organizational Psychology

    Organizational Psychology delves into the science of human behavior in workplace settings. This specialization explores employee motivation, leadership dynamics, organizational development, and performance enhancement. It prepares students to optimize workforce efficiency, manage workplace stress, and foster positive organizational culture, equipping them for roles in HR, corporate consultancy, and workplace well-being.

  • Military Psychology

    Military Psychology focuses on understanding the psychological needs of armed forces personnel, both in combat and peacetime. This specialization includes stress management, trauma counseling, and resilience training for military settings. It cultivates the ability to support mental wellness and strategic performance, making graduates valuable assets in defense services, military hospitals, and research units.

    Know More
  • Counseling Psychology

    Counseling Psychology emphasizes the development of empathetic and solution-oriented approaches to mental wellness. This specialization covers therapeutic techniques, crisis intervention, and mental health advocacy. Students are trained to provide professional guidance in educational institutions, hospitals, NGOs, and private practice, supporting individuals through emotional and psychological challenges.

    Know More
  • Psychology of Space

    A unique and future-forward field, the Psychology of Space explores human behavior in space exploration contexts. This specialization includes astronaut psychology, sensory deprivation, isolation, and group dynamics during missions. It prepares students for careers in space research, psychological support for astronauts, and contributions to space agencies and advanced human factors research.

    Know More
  • Forensic Psychology

    Forensic Psychology bridges psychology and the legal system, focusing on criminal behavior, legal investigations, and court proceedings. This specialization trains students in offender profiling, eyewitness testimony, and rehabilitation strategies. It is ideal for those aspiring to work in law enforcement, legal consultancy, correctional facilities, and criminal investigations.

    Know More
  • Sports Psychology

    Sports Psychology centers on enhancing athletic performance and mental resilience in competitive settings. This specialization covers motivation, performance anxiety, injury recovery, and team dynamics. Students develop the skills to work with athletes, coaches, and sports organizations to strengthen psychological endurance and drive peak performance.

Students explore how psychological principles are applied to improve employee performance, workplace satisfaction, and organizational effectiveness. With a strong foundation in leadership, motivation, and team dynamics, learners can become agents of positive change within various workplace environments.

This specialization nurtures skills essential for managing workforce well-being, enhancing productivity, and driving organizational development.

Key Topics Covered

  • Leadership and Motivation

  • Human Resource Psychology

Military Psychology is designed for students interested in applying psychological principles to defense and national security. It involves the study of mental resilience, performance under stress, and psychological evaluation in military contexts. This specialization offers in-depth insights into managing trauma, combat stress, and behavioral strategies for enhancing soldier preparedness.

It prepares students for roles that support the psychological well-being of armed forces and veterans.

  • Resilience Training and Adaptability
  • Crisis Counseling
  • Human Behavior in Military Environments

  • Crisis Response Coordinator

This specialization opens new frontiers in psychology through its integration with space research and technology.
