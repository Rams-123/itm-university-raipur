Campus Life

Learning never stops, and at ITM IHS, we ensure our students receive the best learning experience possible. Training-Ready, Fully Equipped Campus

Our laboratories are designed to simulate real-world healthcare environments, ensuring students receive practical, job-oriented training. Below are the specialized labs on campus, each equipped with advanced instruments tailored to train future healthcare professionals.

Microbiology Lab
  • Hot Air Oven: Sterilizes laboratory equipment and glassware, eliminating microorganisms to maintain hygiene.
  • Incubator: Provides a controlled environment for the growth of microbial cultures.
  • Water Bath: Maintains samples at a constant temperature for incubation.
  • Compound Microscope: Allows for detailed visualization of microorganisms, cells, and tissues.
Hematology Lab
  • Automated Cell Counter: Quickly and accurately counts blood cells, including RBCs, WBCs, and platelets.
  • Coagulometer: Assesses the coagulation properties of blood by measuring clotting times.
  • Compound Microscope: Examines blood smears to assess cell morphology and detect abnormalities.
Clinical Biochemistry Lab
  • Biochemical Analyzer: Measures specific chemicals and enzymes in biological samples like blood or urine.
  • Electrolyte Analyzer: Determines electrolyte levels - such as sodium, potassium, chloride, and bicarbonate - in biological fluids.
