WHY ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR FEELS LIKE HOME

Living on campus at ITM University, Raipur, means becoming part of a vibrant, supportive community. Designed to feel like home, our hostels provide safe, comfortable, and nurturing accommodation for male and female students on a first-come, first-served basis. Supervised by residential wardens and 24/7 security, the hostels ensure a secure and welcoming environment.

From the moment you step onto our campus, you’ll feel the difference. We’ve designed every corner to create an environment where you can excel academically, embrace new passions, and build lifelong memories. Here’s what makes life at ITM extraordinary:

  • Seamless Connectivity: Our reliable bus services ensure students and staff can travel safely and comfortably, making life on and off campus hassle-free.
  • Comfortable Living Spaces: Our hostels are more than just accommodation – they’re a home away from home. You'll never feel far from comfort with well-furnished twin-sharing rooms, round-the-clock security, housekeeping services, and a mess that serves delicious meals.
  • Round-the-Clock Support: We've got you covered whether you need 24/7 electricity and water or emergency medical assistance with on-call physicians and vehicle support.
  • A Space to Grow: With cutting-edge labs, a resourceful library, and modern classrooms, you’ll have everything you need to fuel your academic journey.
  • Active Lifestyles: Stay fit and have fun with our sports facilities, gym, and indoor recreation spaces. From table tennis to yoga, we ensure you never run out of ways to recharge and relax.

ITMUR STUDENT HOUSE & CLUBS SYSTEM

Student Houses

All students are grouped into five houses named after prominent waterfalls in Chhattisgarh. House allocation is done randomly based on student roll numbers.

Amritdhara
Chitradhara
Ghatarani
Chitrakote
Tirathgarh

Purpose of the House System

  • • Foster a sense of community and belonging
  • • Promote teamwork through club activities and house-based competitions
  • • Develop collaborative and leadership skills
  • • Instill a winning mindset and long-term team spirit

House activities are held on designated Saturdays each semester. The house with the highest achievements is awarded the Running Shield.

Student Clubs

Each student is encouraged to be part of two clubs:

  • • One Departmental Club (based on academic discipline)
  • • One General Club (based on hobbies/interests)

Objectives

  • • Apply classroom learning through practical exposure
  • • Explore and nurture individual passions and talents
  • • Encourage active participation, creativity, and leadership
