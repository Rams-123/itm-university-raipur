ELIGIBILITY AND HOW TO APPLY

To apply for the MCA program at ITM University in Raipur, candidates must hold a degree in Computer Applications, B.Sc. Computer Science, or B.Sc. Mathematics with a minimum of 45% marks. If seats remain vacant, the minimum required percentage may be lowered by 5% with permission from the Vice Chancellor or if Mathematics is a core subject in the Class XII examination.
