Scholarships

ITM Institute of Design and Media offers scholarships to candidates who have demonstrated outstanding academic, social, cultural, or personal achievements. Applicants will be evaluated based on their design skills, creative aptitude, and passion for design, which will guide the final selection process.

The ITM IDM Scholarship Entrance Exam will take place on 14th December, 2024, at the ITM-IDM Andheri Campus. Scholarships will be granted based on academic excellence and financial need, providing talented students from various backgrounds with the opportunity to pursue our programs.

The entrance procedure includes the following stages:

  • Creative Intelligence Test (CIT) - 45 minutes
  • Design Impromptu Test (DIT) - 45 minutes
  • Personal Interview - 20 minutes (Applicants may present their portfolio during this round)

Scholarship Application Deadline: 12th December, 2024
