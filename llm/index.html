ABOUT ITM University Raipur

With a strong academic legacy spanning over 34 years, the ITM Group of Institutions founded ITM University, Raipur in 2012, introducing a progressive and career-focused learning ecosystem to Central India. Strategically located in Raipur,an emerging hub of industry and education,the university offers students a platform to build thriving careers in today’s fast-evolving global economy.

ITM University, Raipur, is known for its future-oriented education, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and an experienced faculty with deep academic and industry roots. The campus fosters development, seamlessly integrating academic rigor with dynamic cultural, technical, and co-curricular activities.

In tune with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, ITM embraces a learning approach emphasizing skills, entrepreneurship, and immersive experiences. From moot courts and simulation-based learning to industry conclaves and thought-provoking debates, students gain hands-on exposure that equips them for real-world challenges.

With diverse programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, is committed to nurturing tomorrow’s innovative, skilled, and purpose-driven leaders.
