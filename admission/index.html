APPLICATION PROCESS

Getting started with your journey at ITM University, Raipur, is simple. Visit our official website, register, and verify your email ID. Complete the online application form, upload the required documents, and pay the application fee. To secure your seat, finalize the process with a ₹15,000 reservation fee.

Once you apply, a dedicated counselor will guide you through every step - from finances and scholarships to campus details and application assistance. For any queries, email us at admissions.raipur@itmuniversity.org with your program details in the subject line.
