ITM BBA (Banking & Finance) (Hons.) 2025-28

The BBA (Banking & Finance) (Hons.) at ITM University, Raipur, is a three-year specialized program with the option for a fourth-year Honors degree under the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. This program is crafted to develop financial acumen, analytical thinking, and practical expertise, preparing students for dynamic careers in banking, fintech, and corporate finance.

  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Equips students with in-depth knowledge of financial systems, banking operations, and investment management aligned with evolving market needs.
  • Integrated On-the-Job Training: Features a 10-month professional training module in the final year to offer hands-on exposure and real-time financial industry experience.
  • Skill-Based Learning: Builds critical skills in data analysis, risk management, and financial planning through workshops, live projects, and simulations.
  • Global Career Orientation: Prepares students for national and international careers in finance, with dedicated placement support and professional certifications.

ELIGIBILITY AND HOW TO APPLY

To apply for the BBA (Banking & Finance) (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, the candidate must have a 10+2 in any discipline with a minimum of 45% marks. If seats remain vacant, the required percentage may be lowered by 5% with approval from the Vice-Chancellor.

ABOUT ITM VOCATIONAL UNIVERSITY

With a proud legacy spanning over 34 years, the ITM Group of Institutions established ITM University, Raipur, in 2012 to redefine higher education in Central India. Nestled in Raipur - an emerging epicenter for industry and education - the university creates a launchpad for students to excel in today’s fast-paced global economy.

ITM University, Raipur, delivers a forward-thinking academic experience supported by cutting-edge infrastructure and a faculty of seasoned educators with academic and industry expertise. Its green, dynamic campus encourages holistic growth, integrating strong academics with vibrant cultural, technical, and co-curricular engagements.

Aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 vision, the university emphasizes practical learning, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Students gain the skills and confidence to lead in their chosen careers through real-world platforms like industry conclaves, moot courts, debates, and hands-on training.

Offering various programs in Management, Law, Engineering, Life Sciences, and International Hospitality, ITM University, Raipur, is committed to shaping future-ready professionals equipped to make a meaningful impact through knowledge, innovation, and leadership.
