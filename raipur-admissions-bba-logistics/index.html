Program Highlights
Industry-Aligned, Future-Ready Curriculum

The BBA (Logistics) (Hons.) program is built in collaboration with logistics industry leaders, ensuring a perfect mix of academic rigor and practical insights.

With modules like Supply Chain Analytics, ERP, and Global Logistics, the curriculum stays aligned with the latest trends and technologies.

Students also get exposure to Quick Commerce, E-commerce, and Warehouse Management, making them industry-ready from day one.

Extensive On-the-Job Training (OJT)

One of the standout features is the immersive one-year On-the-Job Training in top logistics companies.

Students work on real-world logistics operations across e-commerce, automotive, and pharma industries.

This boosts confidence and skills and often leads directly to full-time job offers.

Global Career Opportunities

The logistics and supply chain sector is booming worldwide, and this program prepares students for domestic and international roles.

With subjects covering cross-border logistics, global supply chains, and international business practices, graduates are well-positioned to explore opportunities in MNCs and global markets.

Strong Industry Collaborations and Placement Support

Students benefit from a selective internship process, hands-on industry visits, and mentorship from senior professionals and advisory board members.

A dedicated placement team provides end-to-end career support- from resume building and mock interviews to job search strategy—ensuring strong placement outcomes in top firms.

Skill Development with Financial Support

In addition to soft skills such as communication, leadership, and professional grooming, students are also trained in digital tools, including ERP and logistics software.

During the OJT phase, students also earn a stipend of ₹10,000 to ₹15,000 per month, making this a learning experience that is both financially supportive.

ITM BBA (Logistics) (Hons.) 2025 - 28

The BBA (Logistics) (Hons.) program at ITM University, Raipur, is a specialized three-year undergraduate degree that offers an optional fourth year for an honors degree, as per the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. Designed in collaboration with logistics and supply chain industry experts, this program equips students with domain-specific knowledge and hands-on experience to excel in the rapidly growing logistics sector.

  • Industry-Curated Curriculum: Offers specialized training in supply chain management, e-commerce logistics, warehousing, and ERP systems aligned with global industry practices.
  • One-Year Paid On-the-Job Training (OJT): Students gain real-world exposure during their final year through internships with top companies, earning a monthly stipend while gaining valuable experience.
  • Tech-Integrated Learning: The program includes tools such as logistics software, SAP, and data analytics platforms, preparing students for tech-driven logistics roles.
  • Placement & Career Support: Backed by strong corporate tie-ups and a dedicated placement team, students receive resume support, mentoring, and direct access to career opportunities in logistics, e-commerce, retail, and manufacturing.
