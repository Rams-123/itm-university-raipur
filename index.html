ITM UNIVERSITY, RAIPUR

Established in 2012, ITM University, Raipur, brings over 34 years of expertise in education through the ITM Group of Institutions. Located in the heart of Chhattisgarh's industrial hub, Raipur, the university offers a dynamic environment ideal for students seeking to fast-track their careers.

Aligned with the National Education Policy 2024, ITM University, Raipur, provides skill-based, industry-relevant education to prepare students for the competitive global landscape. The state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections ensure a transformative learning experience.

With numerous awards and recognitions, including NAAC accreditation and rankings for excellence, ITM University, Raipur, is a leading institution in Central India. ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT CELL (EDC)

The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, established the Innovation Cell (MIC) to foster innovation and entrepreneurship in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). MIC promotes the creation of Institution Innovation Councils (IICs) to build a network that supports students in transforming ideas into prototypes and encourages entrepreneurial thinking through diverse programs and collaborations.

Established on May 28, 2021, the Institution Innovation Council (IIC) focuses on leveraging natural and human resources, fostering local collaboration, and promoting gender equality in entrepreneurship. It organizes workshops, hackathons, mentorship programs, and industry partnerships to drive innovation, support startups, and enhance the region's quality of life.

EMPOWERING WOMEN THROUGH TRADITIONAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP

ITM University, Raipur (ITMUR), in collaboration with CII-Yi, is driving a CSR initiative in the village of Uparwara, Naya Raipur. This program empowers the Village Women's Self-Help Group by providing training and support in producing traditional Chhattisgarhi delicacies such as pickles (achar), badi, and papad.

In addition to skill development, the women are guided in marketing their products, with valuable opportunities facilitated by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) and its associates. This initiative fosters women’s empowerment and promotes economic independence.
